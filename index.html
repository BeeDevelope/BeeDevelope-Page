ArıPortal

ArıPortal is a mobile application that is beeing worked on

ArıPortal will only be available for students of Arı Private Schools for now

Right now only an Android version is being worked on but IOS version will also be available at launch

Sorry, we do not have a launch date ETA


Features

A POMODO timer that will have integration with Moodle and a point system

SmartAdmin based automation system integration allows easy wiewing of exam results

Easyly seeing school smart card balance

Upcoming lessons and the food list

Please keep in mind that the final features may not be the same as written here
